Impulse Check 

**Impulse Check** is a personal finance utility app designed to help you pause and reflect before making non-essential purchases. By integrating smart decision-making tools, it empowers users to align their daily spending habits with their long-term financial goals.

## What It Does
Impulse Check acts as a "financial circuit breaker." When you're about to make a purchase, the app analyzes your intent, assesses your current budget status, and provides a "Vibe Score." It helps you differentiate between a necessary expense and an impulsive emotional purchase.

## Key Features
*   **Vibe Score:** Get an instant rating on your potential purchase to see if it fits your current financial health.
*   **Budget Alignment:** See how the purchase impacts your monthly or savings goals in real-time.
*   **Currency Selector:** Fully customizable for global use.
*   **Purchase History Tracker:** Review your past decisions to spot spending patterns and improve future habits.
*   **Savings Goal Integration:** Receive warnings if your current spending threatens your long-term financial targets.
